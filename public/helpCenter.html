Help Center

The Help Center provides assistance to patents, health care professionals, and system users who need support while using the LifeLine Medical Center system.


Our support services include guidance on accessing patient records, scheduling appointments, managing accounts, and navigating the Electronic Health Record (EHR) system.


If you experience technical issues or need further assistance, our support system team is available to help ensure a smooth and efficient healthcare experience.
